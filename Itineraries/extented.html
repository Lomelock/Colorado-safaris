10-Day Extended Safari Expedition

Comprehensive Wildlife & Cultural Experience

Duration: 10 Days / 9 Nights | From: $3,500 per person (based on 6 pax)

Destinations

Arusha • Tarangire • Serengeti • Ngorongoro • Lake Manyara • Selous (Nyerere)

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Arusha

Meet your safari guide at Kilimanjaro Airport and transfer to your hotel in Arusha. Briefing and relaxation at the lodge.

Day 2: Arusha – Tarangire National Park

Drive to Tarangire for game drives through baobab-filled landscapes and elephant herds. Overnight in a lodge inside or near the park.

Day 3: Tarangire – Serengeti National Park

Journey through the Ngorongoro Highlands to Serengeti, enjoying wildlife views along the way. Evening game drive en route to your camp.

Day 4: Serengeti – Full Day Game Drive

Explore central Serengeti in search of lions, leopards, elephants, and the Great Migration (seasonal). Overnight in the heart of the park.

Day 5: Serengeti – Ngorongoro Conservation Area

Early morning game drive, then drive to Ngorongoro. Visit a Maasai village en route and overnight on the crater rim.

Day 6: Ngorongoro Crater – Lake Manyara

Descend into the Ngorongoro Crater for a half-day safari. After lunch, continue to Lake Manyara for overnight stay.

Day 7: Lake Manyara – Arusha – Flight to Selous

Enjoy a morning game drive in Lake Manyara, then return to Arusha. Fly to Selous Game Reserve and transfer to your riverside lodge.

Day 8: Selous – Game Drive & River Safari

Morning game drive followed by an afternoon boat safari on the Rufiji River. Expect to see crocodiles, hippos, and birdlife.

Day 9: Selous – Walking Safari & Relaxation

Begin the day with a guided bush walk. Learn about tracks, insects, and flora. Afternoon at leisure at your lodge or optional game drive.

Day 10: Selous – Return Flight to Arusha / Departure

After breakfast, transfer to airstrip for your return flight to Arusha. Connect to your international departure or onward journey.
