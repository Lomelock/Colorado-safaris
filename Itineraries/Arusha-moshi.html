3-Day Arusha & Moshi Exploration

Discover the Charm of Arusha & Moshi’s Local Culture

Duration: 3 Days / 2 Nights | From: $900 per person (based on 6 pax)

Highlights

  • Arusha National Park
  • Local markets and museums
  • Moshi cultural tours & coffee plantation visits

Destinations: Arusha • Moshi

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Arusha City Tour

Arrive in Arusha and meet your guide. Enjoy a guided tour of the city including the local markets, museums, and cultural sites. Overnight at a hotel in Arusha.

Day 2: Arusha National Park & Transfer to Moshi

Morning game drive or walking safari in Arusha National Park. After lunch, transfer to Moshi for a relaxed evening. Overnight at a lodge in Moshi.

Day 3: Moshi Cultural Tour & Coffee Plantation Visit

Explore the town of Moshi and visit local cultural centers. Enjoy a guided tour of a coffee plantation. Afternoon transfer to the airport or your next destination.
