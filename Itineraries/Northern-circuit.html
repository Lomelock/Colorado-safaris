7-Day Northern Circuit Safari Adventure

Explore Tanzania's Iconic Wildlife & Culture

Duration: 7 Days / 6 Nights | From: $2,100 per person (based on 6 pax)

Destinations

Arusha • Tarangire • Serengeti • Ngorongoro • Lake Manyara

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Arusha

Meet your guide at Kilimanjaro Airport and transfer to your lodge. Rest and prepare for your safari journey.

Day 2: Arusha – Tarangire National Park

Enjoy a scenic drive to Tarangire. Game drive through landscapes rich with elephants and baobabs.

Day 3: Tarangire – Serengeti National Park

Travel through the Rift Valley to the world-famous Serengeti. Game viewing en route to your tented camp.

Day 4: Full-Day in Serengeti

Spend the day on game drives tracking wildlife including big cats, elephants, and (seasonally) the Great Migration.

Day 5: Serengeti – Ngorongoro

Morning drive, optional Maasai village visit, and transfer to Ngorongoro rim with panoramic views.

Day 6: Ngorongoro Crater – Lake Manyara

Descend into Ngorongoro Crater for a Big Five safari, then continue to Lake Manyara for the night.

Day 7: Lake Manyara – Arusha – Departure

Morning safari at Lake Manyara, then return to Arusha and transfer to the airport for your departure.
