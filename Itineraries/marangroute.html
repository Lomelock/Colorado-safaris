7-Day Mount Kilimanjaro Climb via Marangu Route

The Scenic “Coca-Cola” Route to Africa’s Highest Peak

Duration: 7 Days / 6 Nights | From: $4,200 per person (based on 6 pax)

Highlights

  • Climb Mount Kilimanjaro via the scenic Marangu Route (Coca-Cola Route)
  • Experience lush rainforests, moorlands, alpine desert, and glaciers
  • Reach Uhuru Peak (5,895 m) — Africa’s highest summit
  • Overnight in mountain huts with full guide and porter support

Destinations: Kilimanjaro National Park

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Moshi

Arrive at Kilimanjaro International Airport and transfer to Moshi town. Meet your guides for a pre-trek briefing, gear check, and overnight stay at a hotel in Moshi.

Day 2: Moshi to Mandara Hut

Drive to Marangu Gate (1,860 m) and begin the trek through tropical rainforest. Reach Mandara Hut (2,730 m) for your first overnight in a mountain hut. Enjoy scenic views and an early dinner.

Day 3: Mandara Hut to Horombo Hut

Continue the climb through moorland vegetation. Stop for acclimatization and enjoy spectacular views of Kibo and Mawenzi peaks. Arrive at Horombo Hut (3,720 m) for overnight stay.

Day 4: Acclimatization at Horombo Hut

Take a short hike to Zebra Rocks or Mawenzi Valley for acclimatization. Return to Horombo Hut to rest and prepare for higher altitudes. Overnight at Horombo Hut (3,720 m).

Day 5: Horombo Hut to Kibo Hut

Ascend through alpine desert terrain to Kibo Hut (4,700 m), the base for the summit attempt. Spend the day resting and preparing your gear for the midnight climb.

Day 6: Summit Day & Descent to Horombo Hut

Begin the ascent around midnight, reach Uhuru Peak (5,895 m) at sunrise, and celebrate your achievement. Descend via Kibo Hut to Horombo Hut (3,720 m) for overnight stay and rest.

Day 7: Horombo Hut to Marangu Gate & Departure

Descend through the rainforest to Marangu Gate (1,860 m). Receive your summit certificates and transfer back to Moshi town. End of the 7-day Marangu Route adventure.
