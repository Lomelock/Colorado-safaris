3-Day Oldonyo Lengai Mountain Hiking Adventure

A Challenging Trek with Rewarding Vistas

Duration: 3 Days / 2 Nights | From: $1,100 per person (based on 6 pax)

Highlights

  • Hike Oldonyo Lengai, the sacred volcano
  • Stunning crater views and unique lava formations
  • Overnight camping under the stars

Destinations: Oldonyo Lengai

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arusha to Lake Natron

Depart from Arusha and drive toward Lake Natron, located near the base of Oldonyo Lengai. Enjoy scenic landscapes and overnight at a nearby campsite.

Day 2: Hike to the Summit

Start your early morning ascent of Oldonyo Lengai with your guide. Reach the summit by sunrise and enjoy panoramic views of the crater and rift valley. Descend and rest at camp.

Day 3: Return to Arusha

After breakfast and a relaxed morning at the camp, begin your drive back to Arusha, arriving in the late afternoon for drop-off or onward travel.
