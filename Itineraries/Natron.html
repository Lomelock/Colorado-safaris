5-Day Natron Adventure Safari

Experience the Unique Natural Beauty of Lake Natron

Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights | From: $1,700 per person (based on 6 pax)

Highlights

  • Lake Natron's surreal landscapes
  • Flamingo colonies and red-hued waters
  • Visit local salt and mineral villages

Destinations: Lake Natron and surrounding areas

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arusha to Lake Natron

Depart from Arusha and drive through the Great Rift Valley toward Lake Natron. Enjoy scenic stops along the way. Arrive and relax at your lodge near the lake.

Day 2: Lake Natron Exploration

Explore the lake's shoreline, home to thousands of flamingos. Discover the unique red-colored waters and alkaline flats. Visit local Maasai communities.

Day 3: Engare Sero Waterfalls

Take a hike to the Engare Sero Waterfalls. Enjoy a swim and marvel at the towering cliffs. Learn about the area's volcanic geology from your guide.

Day 4: Local Village Visit & Cultural Experience

Spend the day visiting salt-mining villages and engaging in cultural exchanges with the locals. Optional hike around the surrounding volcanic formations.

Day 5: Return to Arusha

After breakfast, drive back to Arusha. Enjoy views of Mount Ol Doinyo Lengai along the way. Arrival in the late afternoon for drop-off or onward travel.
